Project Cosmos

Cosmos is a comprehensive, AI-powered knowledge management platform designed to transform unstructured information into a structured, navigable universe of understanding. The application allows users to precisely Scope their active knowledge base, then leverage tools like Constellations to map relationships and build dynamic knowledge graphs. Users can Query and explore data through customized Discovery configurations, generate insights via Synthesize functions, and interact conversationally with their data using the AI Interface. By applying robust Rules and automation logic, Cosmos empowers users to manage complexity, extract actionable intelligence, and master their collective knowledge efficiently.

Scope

The Scope page is where you define the boundaries of your current exploration. Use this area to select, filter, and constrain the specific subsets of knowledge you wish to work with. Anything you do within the app—from running a query to building a constellation—will only operate within the field of view defined here.

Discovery

The Discovery configuration center allows you to tailor how knowledge is visually presented and navigated. Adjust settings here to configure search result relevance, data visualization preferences, and exploration pathways, ensuring that you can easily uncover insights hidden within the Cosmos in a way that makes sense to you.

Constellations

This is your knowledge mapping workbench. On the Constellations page, you can actively build, visualize, and refine knowledge graphs. Connect disparate pieces of information, map relationships, and sculpt raw data into structured, navigable constellations of understanding.

Synthesize

The Synthesize function is designed to distill vast knowledge into manageable insights. Utilize this page to generate automated summaries, abstract key concepts, or simplify complex information representations, making the core insights of your data readily accessible.

Portal

The Portal page serves as your gateway to the core intelligence of the application. This is where you directly interact with the Cosmos AI using natural language prompts. Use the Portal to ask questions, generate content, and receive dynamic, conversational responses based on your scoped knowledge.

Query

For precise and structured information retrieval, the Query page provides advanced tools. Use structured language or visual query builders here to perform complex, targeted searches across your active knowledge base, allowing for high-precision data retrieval.

Rules

The Rules section allows you to define and manage automation and logic within your system. Configure triggers and conditions here to apply consistent logic across your data—for instance, automatically tagging new information, alerting you to data anomalies, or enforcing governance policies within the Cosmos.
